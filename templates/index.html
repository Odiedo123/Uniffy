About Us

Uniffy is a dynamic mentorship platform built to bridge the gap between Kenyan university students and high school learners seeking career guidance.

We believe that the journey to a fulfilling career begins with the right mentorship — from people who've walked the path before.

At Uniffy, we connect high school students with passionate university mentors who provide real-world insights into various careers, help students explore their interests, and guide them in choosing suitable universities and courses. Whether you're a student confused about career options or simply looking for someone to walk with you on your academic journey, Uniffy makes that connection possible.

Our goal is to empower the next generation with the knowledge, clarity, and confidence they need to shape their future — one guided step at a time.
